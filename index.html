Best Herbs to Grow in Garden

Featured below are the four best herbs to grow in your garden this summer. I hope these herbs will inspire you to whip up wholesome, delicious meals. Cheers!

Four Best Herbs
Parsley sometimes gets a bad rap for being primarily used for garnish but is actually considered a 'superfood'. Go figure!
Basil is a popular herb featured in lots of Italian cuisine. Favorites include pesto, pizza, and the classic insalata caprese.
Cilantro is a fragrant herb that has a distinct fresh taste. It is an integral ingredient in Mexican recipes such as guacamole and pico de gallo.
Mint is a versatile herb that can be used for mint tea when you're not feeling well and a mojito when you are!
